# Business Insights and Recommendations

Project: E-Commerce Sales and Customer Support Analytics

This report summarizes the key insights found from Python analysis, SQL queries, and Power BI dashboard visuals.

## 1. Overall Business Performance

The e-commerce business generated strong overall sales.

Key numbers:

- Total Sales: ₹115M
- Total Profit: ₹26M
- Total Orders: 2500
- Total Customers: 800
- Average Order Value: ₹46K

### Insight

The business has a good revenue base and a healthy profit level. The average order value is also high, mainly because electronics and high-value products contribute strongly to revenue.

### Recommendation

The business should continue focusing on high-value product categories, especially electronics, while also improving customer experience to reduce returns and complaints.


## 2. Monthly Sales Trend

The monthly sales trend showed fluctuations across the year.

### Insight

August had the highest sales compared to other months. Some months such as January, May, and September showed lower sales performance.

### Recommendation

The business should study what caused the August sales increase. If the increase was due to offers, campaigns, or seasonal demand, similar strategies can be used in low-performing months.


## 3. Regional Sales Performance

Region-wise sales showed that North and South regions performed better than other regions.

### Insight

North was the highest revenue-generating region, followed by South. Central region had the lowest sales.

### Recommendation

The company should focus more on Central region through targeted marketing, better product availability, and delivery improvements. Strong regions like North and South should be maintained with good inventory and customer service.


## 4. Product Category Performance

Electronics was the strongest product category by revenue.

### Insight

Most top-selling products were electronics such as laptops and smartphones. Electronics generated much higher revenue compared to other categories because of higher product prices.

### Recommendation

The business should maintain strong inventory for high-performing electronics products. However, it should also analyze low-revenue categories separately by order volume, because some low-price categories may still have good customer demand.


## 5. Top Products Analysis

The top products by sales were mainly Apple, Samsung, HP, Dell, Lenovo, Acer, and MSI electronics products.

### Insight

Premium and branded electronics are the main revenue drivers.

### Recommendation

The company should promote top-selling electronics products during campaigns and ensure these products are available in high-performing regions.


## 6. Return Analysis

The return rate was around 17%.

Major return reasons included:

- Wrong model received
- Wrong item received
- Quality issue
- Not as described
- Size issue
- Late delivery

### Insight

A 17% return rate is significant. Many returns are linked to product mismatch, quality issues, product description problems, and delivery-related problems.

### Recommendation

The company should improve product listing accuracy, seller quality checks, packaging quality, and warehouse picking process. Reducing returns can directly improve profit and customer satisfaction.


## 7. Delivery Delay Analysis

Delayed orders were also visible in the dashboard.

Key numbers:

- Delayed Orders: 771
- Average Delay Days: 1

### Insight

The average delay is only around 1 day, but the number of delayed orders is still high. Xpressbees had the highest delayed orders among delivery partners.

### Recommendation

The company should monitor delivery partner performance regularly. Delivery partners with frequent delays should be reviewed, and better delivery planning should be done for high-demand regions.


## 8. Customer Support Analysis

The support dashboard showed customer complaints and resolution performance.

Key numbers:

- Total Support Tickets: 750
- Open Tickets: 184
- Escalated Tickets: 76
- Average Resolution Days: 5
- Average Satisfaction Score: 3

### Insight

Most support tickets are related to returns, delivery, and refunds. This matches the issues found in return and delivery analysis. The average satisfaction score is moderate, which shows room for improvement.

### Recommendation

The business should improve support response time, refund processing, delivery communication, and return handling. Open and escalated tickets should be monitored daily.


## 9. Connected Business Story

The dashboard shows a clear business story:

Sales are strong, especially in electronics and top regions. However, returns, delivery delays, and support tickets are affecting customer experience.

### Main Business Problem

The company is generating strong sales, but post-purchase issues such as returns, delivery delays, refunds, and support tickets need improvement.

### Main Business Opportunity

If the business reduces returns, improves delivery reliability, and resolves support tickets faster, it can improve customer satisfaction and protect profit.


## Final Recommendations

1. Focus on high-performing electronics products.
2. Improve sales in the Central region using targeted campaigns.
3. Reduce product returns by improving seller quality checks and product listing accuracy.
4. Monitor delivery partner delays, especially high-delay partners.
5. Improve packaging and warehouse picking to reduce wrong item and damaged product issues.
6. Speed up refund and return support processes.
7. Track open and escalated tickets daily.
8. Use dashboard KPIs regularly to monitor sales, returns, delivery, and support performance.


## Final Conclusion

This project helped analyze e-commerce sales, product performance, delivery issues, returns, and customer support problems.

The final dashboard helps business users understand overall performance, identify problem areas, and take data-driven decisions.
